Current Members

Business collection is shared — everyone with Business access sees the same inventory, sales, and tax data. Personal collections are always private.

Adding a New Member

Accounts are created manually in Supabase. To add someone:

  1. Go to your Supabase dashboard → Authentication → Users
  2. Click Invite user and enter their email
  3. They'll get an email to set their password
  4. They sign in at startingfivecollectibles.vercel.app
Business vs Personal: The Business / Mine toggle in the sidebar controls which collection is shown. Andrew will see the shared Business collection and his own Personal — same as you.
